Services

Pre-Audit

A comprehensive pre-audit takes place on site and off site. This includes, but is not limited to reviewing current policies, site buildings, law compliance, health and safety compliance, risk assessments, employee training etc.

Policy Creation

All current company policies (policies 1-8) are reviewed. Policies are either created to replace or to add to current policies. All policies provided by Ethical Compliance are tailored and personalised to your company.

Non Compliance Correction

All non-compliances and current status of the company will be reviewed. All non-compliances will be discussed with management and strategies created to correct these.

Training

Ethical Compliance can train every facet of your company - from management to administrative staff, agri-workers to drivers. Training in all aspects of compliance is offered. We run SIZA training for all staff members to ensure that all employees, and the company as a whole, are up to date with the latest legislation.

Workshops

Workshops are tailored to your companies needs. Our workshops include: Basic SIZA legislation, SIZA version 6, agri-worker committees, company policy "check and create" guidelines.
